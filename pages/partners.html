What its Shape Memory Technology?

Shape Memory allows aligners to always return to their initial shape and adapt perfectly to the patient's mouth when reaching average body temperature
(98.6º F / 37ºC).
Aligners made from conventional thermoplastics sheets have heavy initial force, which overstrains the teeth causing pain then rapidly decreases to less than the appropriate force, and as a result, cannot maintain orthodontic force, making it difficult to obtain the intended orthodontic results.
