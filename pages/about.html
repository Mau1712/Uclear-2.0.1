Meet our founders!

Ricardo Polanco

CEO & Founder Uclear®
Doctor of Dental Surgery

He was born in Chicago (USA), raised in Caracas (Venezuela) where he received his Dentist degree from the Central University of Venezuela in 2002. He received continued education and training in several fields of dentistry in different countries. In 2018 he finished his education and training at the University of Miami & Jackson Memorial Hospital, and finally, he was recognized as DDS (Doctor in Dental Surgery) in the United States of America. More than 20 years of experience in private practice. Right now, he is based in Miami, Florida, in private practice at Polpa Dental. Member of the ADA and FDA in the United States. National and international speaker in different areas of dentistry. Entrepreneur in innovative products and protocols in dentistry always keeping his education up to date.

Richard Rodriguez

CEO & Founder Uclear®
Dentist, Scientist & Researcher

Graduated dentist from the Central University of Venezuela, the best dental university in the country. Clinical and scientific advisor & professor of implant dentistry in Boston, MA. Developer and researcher in materials of dental implants, protocols and strategies in the field of digital dentistry. Cadcam specialist. Trained at Harvard, Japan, South Korea, Germany and Colombia.
